Task 2 :Write your letter to your future self (at least 90 words). Address it as if you are writing to yourself a few years from now. Include the following elements in your letter.

1. Begin with a warm greeting and an acknowledgment of your present self.
2. Mention specific goals and dreams you hope to achieve in the future.
3. Reflect on the challenges you might encounter in the pursuit of your goals.
4. Offer words of encouragement and motivation. Share any advice you believe will help your future self stay confident.
5. Express your confidence in your future self's abilities and potential.
6. Conclude the letter with a heartfelt closing, sign your name, and date it.
